Coutto Pattern is Shadow Stitch's sewing pattern tool for projector cutting, stitching, printing, managing, and storing patterns in one app.

With digital precision calibration, Coutto projects patterns at actual size onto fabric so users can cut directly along the projected lines and simplify the cutting workflow. It supports common pattern formats including PLT, DXF, PDF, SVG, PNG, JPG, and WebP, with A4/A3 tiled printing, PDF pattern stitching, image pattern size calibration, and organized storage by style and format.

Coutto also includes a sewing community where makers can share ideas and discover designers, patterns, tutorials, fabrics, and notions.
